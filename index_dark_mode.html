About me

My name is Benjamin Dourthe. I am a passionate Data Scientist with 10 years of multidisciplinary experience in Computer Modeling, Advanced Analytics and Artificial Intelligence. I am also passionate about learning, technology, puzzle-solving and using science and out-of-the-box thinking to make a difference in the world.

I grew up in France, where I graduated from a Master's in Mechanical Engineering and a Master of Science, Technology and Health back in 2013. Then, I studied and worked in Belgium for 3 years as a Research Engineer and graduated from a PhD in Biomedical Engineering and Data Science in 2017. Since then, I have been living and working in Canada and became a permanent resident in 2019.

My life-long objective is to constantly expand and diversify my skillset to build innovative and accessible platforms that will enhance our understanding of science and help identify usable patterns in data.

Over time, I have acquired a solid programming experience by developing algorithms for various types of application: artificial intelligence, computer vision, natural language processing, medical imaging, biomedical engineering, werables, medical devices, finance, public health, sportswear, information, and more. I believe to be a creative thinker who is strongly committed to his passion and objectives. I also have demonstrated my ability to quickly learn and adapt to new fields of knowledge, teams and work environments.

In summary, I am a big dreamer who never walks away from a challenge and has the best intentions to contribute to the betterment of society.

CURRENT LOCATION: Toronto, ON, Canada
