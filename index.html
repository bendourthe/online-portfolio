About me

Hi there! My name is Benjamin Dourthe. I am a Data Scientist and Biomechanical Engineer with a passion for research and innovation.

I am originally from France, where I graduated from a professional Mechanical Engineering program (ENIM) and a Master of Science, Technology and Health (University of Lorraine) back in 2013. After my graduation, I studied and worked in Belgium for 3 years, where I graduated from a PhD in Biomedical Sciences (KU Leuven) in 2017. Since then, I have been living and working in Canada.

During my time as an academic, I have been leading a variety of research projects and collaborated with many industry partners (Adidas, Dr. Scholl's, Orpyx Medical Technologies Inc., Curv). Over the years, I have acquired a solid experience in developing algorithms (Python, Matlab) for various types of application (medical imaging, computer modeling, image and signal processing, machine learning, computer vision, app and web development). I also have a strong understanding of the human physiology and hope to be given the chance to share my unique skillset and knowledge to contribute to the development of cutting-edge technologies that will improve people's health, performance and quality of life.
