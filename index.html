About me

My name is Benjamin Dourthe. I am a multidisciplinary data scientist with 9+ years of experience in research, algorithm development and data analysis. I am also passionate about learning, technology, puzzle-solving and using science and out-of-the-box thinking to make a difference in the world.

I grew up in France, where I graduated from a Master's in Mechanical Engineering and a Master of Science, Technology and Health back in 2013. Then, I studied and worked in Belgium for 3 years as a Research Enginner and graduated from a PhD in Biomedical Engineering and Data Science in 2017. Since then, I have been living and working in Canada, where I became a permanent resident.

My life-long objective has been to learn and diversify my skillset to build innovative and accessible platforms that will enhance our understanding of science and help indentify patterns in data (especially in healthcare).

Over time, I have acquired a solid programming experience by developing algorithms in Python and Matlab for various types of application (medical imaging processing, computer modeling, signal processing, machine learning, computer vision). I believe to be a creative thinker who is strongly committed to his passion and objectives. I also have demonstrated my ability to quickly learn and adapt to new teams and work environments.

In summary, I am a big dreamer who never walks away from a challenge and has the best intentions to contribute to the betterment of society.

CURRENT LOCATION: Toronto, ON, Canada
