About me

I am an AI Strategist and Machine Learning Leader with over 10 years of expertise navigating the complexities of digital health, MedTech, and regulated environments. I specialize in building and scaling intelligent systems, from first-in-class wearables and implantables to advanced imaging solutions, guiding them from initial prototype to clinical-grade product.

My technical foundation combines deep expertise in Algorithm Development, Signal Processing, Data Science, Computer Vision and Machine Learning, with a strong focus on engineering discipline. I am currently pioneering the rollout of Agentic and Generative AI platforms, establishing training, governance and security frameworks that enable team-wide access to AI tools, while ensuring compliance with regulatory standards and data privacy regulations.

I define myself by fast, robust iteration and cross-functional leadership. Whether aligning software engineering with clinical goals or automating complex data pipelines, I am passionate about bridging the gap between cutting-edge research and accessible, life-saving technologies.

At my core, I am a big dreamer who never walks away from a challenge, committed to leveraging innovation for the betterment of society.
